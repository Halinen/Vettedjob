\noindent\textit{Plasma and Microwave System Design:}
\begin{itemize}[noitemsep, topsep=2pt, leftmargin=*]
    \item Designed TM$_{012}$-mode coaxial-tuned resonant cavity from first
          principles (HFSS, COMSOL); optimised EM field distribution for

          enlarged effective plasma and deposition area to 75\,mm diameter

          on 60\,mm Si substrate (heatsink / optical window grade)
    \item Designed complete RF transmission chain: low-loss stub-tuned
          coaxial transitions, waveguide sealing windows, and adjustable
          sliding shorts with choke structures for impedance tuning
\end{itemize}
